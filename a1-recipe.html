Marinated Chicken Fajitas

By: Daniel Flynt

Ingredients:

  • 1 box of RICE A RONI Spanish Rice
  • 3 tablespoons of butter
  • 1 Large green pepper
  • 1 Large yellow or white onion
  • 2 medium-large chicken breasts
  • 1 Bottle of MOJO Criollo marinade
  • 6 Flour tortillas
  • 2 Cups of water
  • 1 Del Monte 14oz. can of diced tomatoes with green chilies
  • 1 head of iceberg lettuce
  • 1 jar of Herdez Salsa Verde
  • 1 bag of Sargento 4 Cheese Mexican Blend
  • Tapotio, Cholula, or preferred hot sauce

Directions:

The night before you plan to cook this, cut and clean your chicken and place it in a ziploc bag. Pour the MOJO marinade into the bag until the chicken is completely covered. Put it in the refrigerator for about 24 hours. Remember to shake the bag to redistribute all of the spices. Add the 2 tablespoons of butter to a medium sized frying pan set to medium heat. Follow the directions on the back of the RICE A RONI box. Once your rice is covered, set a timer for 20 minutes and reduce the heat to low. Add the remaining tablespoon of butter to a pan and season your green pepper and onion strips with salt, freshly ground black pepper, garlic powder, and Mexican Chile powder. Set the heat to medium and stir when necessary. While your green pepper and onion are becoming best friends in the pan of love, begin cooking your chicken on the grill. Begin cleaning your lettuce while everything is cooking. Cook your green pepper and onion as desired and wrap them in aluminum foil with your chicken once the chicken has been cooked thoroughly. Place your tortillas, or if you have a tortilla warmer, place them in that with a damp paper towel in your handy dandy microwave and heat for 35-40 seconds on High.
